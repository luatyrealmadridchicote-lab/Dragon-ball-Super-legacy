# Gameplay Guide
# Dragon Ball Super Legacy

## Getting Started

### Character Selection

You can choose from 7 different characters:

1. **Goku** - Balanced fighter with best transformation options
   - Pros: Strong transformations, versatile attacks
   - Cons: Medium defense

2. **Vegeta** - Powerful Saiyan with high attack
   - Pros: High attack power, Blue transformation
   - Cons: Lower intelligence-based abilities

3. **Gohan** - Hybrid with high potential
   - Pros: Adaptable, good at range
   - Cons: Lower starting power

4. **Krillin** - Human warrior with tech abilities
   - Pros: Destructo Disc, Kienzan
   - Cons: Lower health

5. **Trunks** - Time traveler with balanced stats
   - Pros: Future tech, Sword arts
   - Cons: Limited transformations

6. **Jiren** - Universe 11 champion
   - Pros: Extreme power, Perfect form
   - Cons: Limited growth potential

7. **UI Goku** - Ultra Instinct master
   - Pros: Highest power, all abilities
   - Cons: Requires high level unlock

### Story Mode Progression

The game progresses through 7 main sagas:

1. **Dragon Ball Era (Tutorial)**
   - Learn basic controls
   - Meet Master Roshi
   - Battle weak enemies
   - Power Level: 1,000-5,000

2. **Saiyan Invasion**
   - Fight Raditz, Nappa, Vegeta
   - Unlock first transformations
   - Power Level: 5,000-30,000

3. **Namek Journey**
   - Travel to Planet Namek
   - Discover new abilities
   - Face Frieza
   - Power Level: 30,000-100,000

4. **Android & Cell Saga**
   - Androids arrival
   - Perfect Cell emerges
   - Tournament
   - Power Level: 100,000-250,000

5. **Majin Buu Terror**
   - Buu's forms
   - Fusion techniques
   - Spirit Bomb climax
   - Power Level: 250,000-500,000

6. **Dragon Ball Super**
   - New gods of power
   - Goku Black threat
   - Blue transformation mastery
   - Power Level: 500,000-1,000,000

7. **Tournament of Power**
   - Face all universes
   - Jiren encounter
   - Ultra Instinct awakening
   - Power Level: 1,000,000+

### Combat System

**Basic Attacks**
- Left Click: Normal strike
- Sneak + Click: Charged attack
- Double Click: Combo attack

**Ki Abilities**
- Sneak + Jump + Click: Ki Blast (20 Ki)
- Sneak + Space: Ability menu

**Transformations**
- Crouch + Sprint: Transform
- Requirements: Power level thresholds

**Special Moves**
- Kamehameha (100 Ki) - Signature attack
- Spirit Bomb (150 Ki) - Ultimate technique
- Destructo Disc (80 Ki) - Piercing attack
- Fusion (requires ally) - Combine power

### Energy System

**Ki Energy**
- Max: 200
- Regenerates: 5 per second when not moving
- Faster regen when meditating

**Ability Costs**
- Ki Blast: 20
- Normal Attack: 5-10
- Kamehameha: 100
- Spirit Bomb: 150
- Destructo Disc: 80

### Training

**With Master Roshi**
- Gain 50 EXP per session
- Duration: 5 minutes
- Power Bonus: +25
- Available: Early game

**With King Kai**
- Gain 100 EXP per session
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Power Bonus: +50
- Available: Mid game

**With Whis**
- Gain 200 EXP per session
- Duration: 15 minutes
- Power Bonus: +100
- Available: Late game

### Items & Equipment

**Healing Items**
- Senzu Bean: Restore full health
- Super Potion: Restore 50% health
- Regular Potion: Restore 25% health

**Power-ups**
- Power Pill: +500 Power Level (temporary)
- God Ki Fragment: Unlock god forms
- Divine Water: Permanent +10% stats

**Equipment**
- Gi Armor: Basic defense
- Weighted Gear: +Power, -Speed
- God Robe: +Defense, +Ki Regen

### Transformations Guide

**Super Saiyan (SSJ)**
- Requirement: 50,000 Power Level
- Bonus: +50% Attack, +50% Speed
- Duration: Until defused
- Ki Cost: 50 per 10 seconds

**Super Saiyan 2 (SSJ2)**
- Requirement: 100,000 Power Level + SSJ
- Bonus: +70% Attack, +70% Speed, Lightning effects
- Duration: Until defused
- Ki Cost: 75 per 10 seconds

**Super Saiyan 3 (SSJ3)**
- Requirement: 250,000 Power Level + SSJ2
- Bonus: +100% Attack, +80% Speed, +50% Defense
- Duration: Until defused
- Ki Cost: 100 per 10 seconds

**Super Saiyan Blue (SSB)**
- Requirement: 500,000 Power Level + God Ki
- Bonus: +150% Attack, +100% Speed, Blue aura
- Duration: Until defused
- Ki Cost: 125 per 10 seconds

**Ultra Instinct (UI)**
- Requirement: 1,000,000 Power Level + All forms
- Bonus: +200% Speed, +50% Defense, +100% Ki Regen
- Duration: 30 seconds
- Ki Cost: 200 per activation

### Fusion System

**Fusion Dance**
- Requires 2 players nearby
- Duration: 5 minutes
- Power Multiplier: 1.5x
- Cooldown: 1 minute

**Potara Fusion**
- Requires 2 players with Potara Earrings
- Duration: 5 minutes
- Power Multiplier: 1.7x
- Cooldown: 2 minutes

**Fused Names**
- Goku + Vegeta = Vegito
- Goku + Krillin = Gokillin
- Trunks + Gohan = Transhan

### Boss Battle Tips

**Before Battle**
- Prepare healing items
- Equip best gear
- Check power level difference
- Train if underpowered

**During Battle**
- Dodge enemy attacks
- Build up ki for special moves
- Use type advantages
- Heal when needed

**Boss Strategies**
- **Raditz**: Use Kamehameha
- **Vegeta**: Fusion recommended
- **Frieza**: Build power, use Spirit Bomb
- **Cell**: Use Kamehameha
- **Buu**: Fusion + Spirit Bomb combo
- **Jiren**: Use Ultra Instinct

### Achievement System

**Combat Achievements**
- First Victory: Win first battle
- Boss Slayer: Defeat first boss
- Champion: Defeat all bosses
- Unstoppable: Power level over 9,000

**Transformation Achievements**
- Golden Warrior: Unlock SSJ
- Electric Anger: Unlock SSJ2
- Long Hair: Unlock SSJ3
- Divine Power: Unlock Blue
- Instinct Master: Unlock Ultra Instinct

**Progression Achievements**
- Saga Completion: Finish each saga
- Collector: Find all Dragon Balls
- Trainer: Complete all training types
- Fusion Master: Perform 5 fusions

## Tips & Tricks

1. Train regularly - Power level increases are essential
2. Farm bosses on lower difficulty for experience
3. Save healing items for boss battles
4. Use fusion for difficult battles
5. Transformations drain Ki - manage carefully
6. Different characters have unique advantages
7. NPC dialogue contains story hints
8. Complete side missions for bonus rewards

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I unlock transformations?**
A: Reach required power level and saga progress

**Q: Can I change characters mid-game?**
A: Only at checkpoints/save points

**Q: What's the max power level?**
A: 999,999,999

**Q: How do I fuse?**
A: Stand near another player and use /fusion command

**Q: Where are Dragon Balls?**
A: Collect from defeated bosses and hidden locations
